    RULES:

  • This is a 2 Player Game.
  • Take turns typing which column you'd like to drop your piece into.
  • Press your enter key or the button marked "Enter" to place your piece.
  • Once one person has gotten 5 pieces in a row (across, up/down, or diagonally), they are the winner!
